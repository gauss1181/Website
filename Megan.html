Welcome to my home page!

My name is Megan. I am a second-year undergraduate student in the Mellon College of Science (MCS) at Carnegie Mellon University, double majoring in mathematics and computer science. I am an extremely kinesthetic, hands-on person who loves to learn by freely designing things. My main areas of interest include game development, web application development, front-end user interface design, theoretical mathematics, and algorithms/complexity analysis. I'm also passionate about education and improving others' quality of learning. In my spare time, outside of homework, I enjoy video games, logic puzzles (especially Sudoku), Rubik's cubing, taking walks around the city, competition programming, trivia, and hackathons.
