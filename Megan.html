Welcome to my home page!

My name is Megan Chen. I am a second-year undergraduate student in the Mellon College of Science (MCS) at Carnegie Mellon University, double majoring in mathematics and computer science. My main areas of interest include theoretical math, algorithmic analysis and design, and web app design. I'm also passionate about education and improving others' quality of learning. This past semester, I've held on-campus jobs at Carnegie Mellon as a peer tutor in the Academic Development Office, and a Concepts of Mathematics (21-127) grader/course assistant. In addition, I am thrilled to announce that I will be serving as a Teaching Assistant (TA) for the mathematics course Integration, Differential Equations, and Approximation (21-122, Calculus II) for the Spring 2014 semester. In my spare time, outside of homework, I enjoy video games, logic puzzles (especially Sudoku), Rubik's cubing, taking walks around the city, competition programming, trivia, and hackathons.
